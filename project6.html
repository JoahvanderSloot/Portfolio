Kuru Kuru Kururin Discription

Kuru Kuru Kururin is a puzzle-action game where you navigate a constantly rotating flying machine through maze-like levels. You have to time your rotation to move through thin spaces avoid obstacles and reach the goal.

In my version of this game, I added powerups and checkpoints. The original already has healing pads, which are also in my version.

The powerups I added are randomly chosen when you fly over the powerup pickup. You can get one of three following powerups:
- Speed
- Getting small
- Shield

My game has five levels which all get progressively harder and introduce new features at each level, with the first one being very simple without any extra features and the final level being a real challenge with multiple of the features mentioned before.

This game is made using Unity and C#, released in November 2023 and took 1 week to make.
