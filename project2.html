Disco Dungeon Discription

In this game you are an explorer in a dungeon, trying to collect treasure. This dungeon is not a normal dungeon though, it is disco-themed, with a dance floor changing color on the beat, and you can only move on the beat as well.

You have a pickaxe and a sword. The pickaxe has five uses and you can use it to mine walls in the dungeon, so you can get to treasure faster. The sword is used to fight off the orcs that roam the dungeon.

These orcs will walk towards you and damage you when they hit you, if you hold out your sword and they are in range to see it they will walk away. When two orcs hit each other they will grow into a big red orc, these have two lives instead of the one lives from the normal green orcs. The red orcs will also not get scared away by your sword. When I red orc hits another orc it will eat it and heal back up to 2 hearts if you hit it before in the level.

Every time you go up a level the dungeon gets bigger. The dungeon is a grid that randomly generates, the walls orcs and treasure spawn randomly too. If you donâ€™t like the music or sound effects you can turn these off in the main menu. It also tracks your high score.

This game is made using Unity and C#, and released in April 2024.
