About Me

Hello!
I am Joah, I am a 17-year-old game development student from the Netherlands.

I am very passionate about creating games, and I also enjoy playing them.
From designing simple games my friends and I used to play in elementary school, to coding video games myself, my journey in game development has been a long and creative one. I'm an avid gamer, movie enthusiast, and book lover, which all inspire my passion for storytelling and fuel my creativity.
Outside the virtual world, I’m active in the gym and train in kickboxing. I enjoy solving Rubik’s cubes, exploring nature and I love animals. I am also fascinated with the samurai and ancient Japan, with its rich history and culture.

Projects

WA-LCH

A twin-stick-shooter game where you play as a cleaning robot with a task to clean out an abandoned laboratory. You have to defend yourself against aggressive robots while trying to clean out radioactive waste and turn on the power generators.

Shy

A short puzzle game where you have to navigate through rooms while trying to avoid touching other people. Since your social anxiety will make you crumble under this interaction.

Disco Dungeon

A infinitely playable game where you move around a disco-themed dungeon, on the beat of the music. And you try to collect treasure while avoiding the dangerous orcs attacking you.

SteamBugs

A classic “Shoot them up” with a steampunk twist. You fly through an abandoned factory, while you get attacked by robot insects. You need to destroy these and try to get to the end boss, wich returns with new attacks after you killed it for a few phases.

Asteroids 3D

A clone of the original "Asteroids" game, but then with 3D sprites. You can collect coins in space, which allows you to buy better ships. In the tenth round, an enemy ship appears to battle you.

Kuru Kuru Kururin

A new version of the old "Kuru Kuru Kururin" game, with a few new twists. Navigate trough the levels and find different useful points and powerups trough the five levels.
