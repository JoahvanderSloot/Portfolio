Asteroids 3D Discription

In my take on the old “Asteroids” game, but with 3D assets, is gameplay-wise very similar to the original. You have a top-down view of your spaceship and you can fly around the screen shooting asteroids with break up into smaller pieces or space rubble. Every time you clear all the asteroids a new round starts and new space rocks get spawned in. Every round a yellow sphere also spawns which gives you 10 coins to spend in the shop. Every round you also have a chance for there to spawn a wrench which when you pick it up can be used to fix damage to your ship.

Breaking asteroids gives you points that get added up to your score, also a high score is tracked. In the shop mentioned earlier, you have 4 different spacecrafts. The first one is the default ship and is unlocked at the start of the game. The other 3 have to be bought with coins. These spacecrafts have different attacks which all have their unique uses.

These attacks are very important because in the 10th round, an enemy ship spawns in and tries to kill you. When you defeat this ship you get points and coins.

This game is made using Unity and C#, and released in February 2024.
