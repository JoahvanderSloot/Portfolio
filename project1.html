Shy Game Discription

In this game you are a socially awkward character, you have to navigate around rooms, unlock doors, and try to escape. All without touching anyone, since if you do you will crumble under the embarrassment.

You can move your character around by clicking somewhere in the level. It uses the Unity NavMesh to calculate its path and walk there.

It has three small levels, it has a levels menu where it keeps track of which levels you cleared already. I added a settings menu where you can turn on or off the sound, you can change your camera sensitivity and you can change the color of your character (the NPCâ€™s get the opposite color of your character automatically).

This game is made using Unity and C#, and released in May 2024.
